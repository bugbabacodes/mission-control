## Notes for Outreach

### Messaging Framework:
1. **Personalized hook** - Reference their specific funding, launch, or post
2. **Credibility signal** - Mention similar projects/experience
3. **Value proposition** - Focus on "0 to 1" execution for pre-seed
4. **Clear CTA** - Offer free consultation or technical audit

### Red Flags to Avoid:
- No mention of budget or funding
- Unfocused scope ("Idea guys")
- Unrealistic timelines
- No clear use case

### Follow-up Strategy:
- Day 1: Initial outreach
- Day 3: Follow up with relevant case study
- Day 7: Final follow up with social proof
- Day 14: Add to nurture sequence
