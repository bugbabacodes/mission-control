## Target Profile (ONLY these qualify)
- **Company Stage:** Series A or later, OR funded startups with $5M+ raised
- **Company Size:** 20-200 employees
- **Industry:** SaaS, Fintech, AI/ML, Web3, B2B platforms
- **Location:** US, UK, EU, India, Singapore, UAE
- **Decision Maker:** CTO, VP Engineering, Head of Product, CEO (at small cos)

## DISQUALIFY (never target)
- Individual freelancers/solopreneurs
- Agencies/consultancies (they sell services, don't buy them)
- Non-tech companies (restaurants, retail, etc.)
- Pre-seed/no funding (unless YC/Techstars tier)
- Companies <10 people or >1000 people

## Lead Sources (priority order)
1. Y Combinator portfolios (latest batches)
2. Techstars alumni
3. Recent funding announcements (Crunchbase)
4. LinkedIn job posts (hiring engineers = have budget)
5. Conference speaker lists (ReactConf, KubeCon, etc.)

## Outreach Strategy

- Personalized first line mentioning specific detail
- No mass templated spam
